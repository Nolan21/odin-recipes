Steak

Description

This steak is fillet mignon. One of the best, if not the best cuts possible.

Make sure you savor the taste because it expensive! ($40/pound)

Ingredients

  • 1/2 Pound of Fillet Mignon
  • Butter
  • Salt
  • Pepper
  • Iron Skillet

Steps

  1. Melt butter on skillet at medium-low heat
  2. Salt & Pepper your meat to taste. Both sides.
  3. Pan sear it on your cast iron skillet.
  4. Medium heat. 8 min both sides = Medium rare.
