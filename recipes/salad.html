Salad

Description

This is a basic salad. Make sure you eat salad regualrly to stay healthy.

Not only will you get some essential vitamins and minerals, but it will help with your digestion.

Ingredients

  • Your favorite salad type (ex. Arugula)
  • Chopped onions, green pepper, cucumber
  • Carrot shavings
  • Crutons
  • Black olives
  • Your favorite dressing. Better be Ranch!

Steps

  1. Fill bowl with salad
  2. Chop your veggies of choice into smaller than bite size pieces.
  3. Shave them carrots
  4. Toss in some crutons
  5. Toss in some olives
  6. Slather it up in some nice dressing and you're done!
